About Us


Hey everyone! We are Phil and Kathy Smith. We became missionaries in Guatemala in February of 2018. We are following the plan God has for our lives by spreading His Word and Love across the nation together.

We first met at Liberty Bible College in 1976. Years later we rekindled our friendship through Facebook and soon began dating and we married in 2015.

Phil is a retired Attorney as well as a Pastor, and Kathy is a retired Registered Nurse.We have 6 kids and 10 grand kids between us whom all live in the states. We miss them dearly! We plan to use the gifts God has given us to help those in need as well as spread God’s Love through Guatemala and it’s surrounding areas.

He said to them, “Go into all the world and preach the gospel to all creation.” –Mark 16:15

Carry each other’s burdens, and in this way you will fulfill the law of Christ. –Galatians 6:2
